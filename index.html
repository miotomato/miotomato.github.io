COMPUTATIONAL BIOLOGIST AND DATA SCIENTIST

Turning complex biological data into meaningful insights.

I am a computational biologist and postdoctoral researcher at the University of Virginia in Chongzhi Zang's lab, with 10 years of research experience in bioinformatics and computational biology.

My work focuses on using statistical modeling, machine learning, multi-omics integration, and scalable bioinformatics pipelines to extract biological insights from high-dimensional datasets. I am currently seeking computational biologist or data scientist roles in pharmaceutical and biotech settings, where I can contribute strong analytical, computational, and biological interpretation skills to support drug discovery, translational research, and human health.
